# Progress Discipline

Treat `PLAN.md` as the plan of record and `PROGRESS.md` as the execution log.

## Workflow

1. Update `PLAN.md` when task structure, priority, or dependency changes.
2. Update `PROGRESS.md` when work starts, changes direction, completes, or hands off.
3. Keep entries short and specific with owner, paths, current objective, validation, and next step.

## Rules

- Do not duplicate long text across both files.
- Do not start substantial edits without an in-progress note.
- Do not hand off work without naming the next owner and next step.

## Do Not Use

Do not use as a substitute for implementation planning. Pair with `task-slicing` when the work itself is not yet structured.
